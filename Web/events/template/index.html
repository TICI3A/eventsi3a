Arrival

By train

Zaragoza has a high-speed connection to Madrid and Barcelona (80-90 minutes). You can check the available connections and buy your train tickets on the RENFE website.

By bus

Zaragoza has an excellent connection with the rest of Spain by bus. You can check the available connections and buy your bus tickets on the Zaragoza Central Bus Station website.

By plane

Zaragoza airport has national and international connections, being 20 minutes from the Río Ebro Campus by taxi. There is a bus every 30 minutes.Taxis

Taxis

There are taxi ranks at the airport and the train / bus stations. You can also call a taxi at these telephones: (976 42 42 42) / (75 976 75 75).

By car

Through the Z40 in both directions passing through the North of Zaragoza, you must enter through the exit that indicates Campus Río Ebro. In both cases you will come to a roundabout and you must take the first exit to the right, continue along the street without leaving it until you see the Río Ebro Campus.
GPS coordinates: Latitude 41.684815, -0.887275 Length

Public transport

The Río Ebro Campus / I + D Building can be reached by public transport. The best option is the tram line that connects the north with the south of Zaragoza, and reaches the Río Ebro Campus passing through the entire Center of our city. The urban bus lines also reach the Río Ebro Campus. Full schedules and itineraries can be checked at Urbanos de Zaragoza.
